About Me

I'm Vik, a London-based photographer and videographer working with brands, businesses and organisations to create visual content that shows the people behind what you do.

Before going freelance, I spent several years working in-house producing content across a range of industries. This included the automotive sector with Topaz Detailing, as well as financial services with Sedulo, a Top 100 UK professional services firm. Alongside this, I worked across events, sport and hospitality, capturing everything from corporate conferences and gala dinners to brand campaigns and live events.

Working inside businesses gave me a strong understanding of how visual content needs to perform, not just creatively, but commercially. My focus is always on creating work that feels natural, engaging and reflective of the people behind the organisation.

As a full-time freelance creative, I work closely with clients from brief to delivery, keeping the process straightforward while ensuring the final work genuinely stands out.

If you're planning something and need strong visuals to support it, I'd love to hear about it.
